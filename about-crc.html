ABOUT CRC

 

CRC is a robotics competiton being held in Montreal since 2001. Every year there is a new competiton where new taskes have to be completed. The founders of CRC were not happy with the competitions out there. CRC is all about bilingualism, multidisciplinary, teamwork as well as equity. As some of the other competitions dont mind your mentors building your robot CRC is all students. At CRC its not just robots, its everything from programing to creating a kiosk, website,video, tutorial, presentation to robotics competitions. You dont have to be a "nerd" to participate in CRC.

 

 

Starting in early fall there is a kickoff where the tasks, teams kits and information for the year is realised to the public. After the kickoff most teams start right away to prototype a robot for the year. There are programing competitons montly. On the competition days you have lots of work to do. Creating your kiosk, setting up your workspace, doing the finishing touches on your robot to practicing on the practice feild. The in person programing competition, the videos being played and everything else that happends at CRC.

ABOUT TAKTIK 2025

 

 

 

About the Game

This 2025 CRC game is called Tak Tik. The game is 2 teams vvs 2 teams. The game peices are rings with a hallow middle, The teams have to use their colored game peice.You canot have rigns touching your robot before the game starts. The game is 5 minutes.

About the Feild

You can grab rins form the stack or the hook. The stack is a pile of 14 rigns stacked onto each other. The hook has 2 horisontal bars with 8 rigns on each bar. There is 4 diffrent areas where a team can score on. There is the "ring cachter","the inversed post", the "duel" and the "zone". The feild is devided into a 3 by 3 grid. To get a TikTakToe you need to have 3 posts you own. To own a post your ring has to be the last one placed, exept the inversed post is the first team that places their ring in the post. If you get a tiktaktoe the values of the posts incress by 50%. There is 4 posts, 2 inverted posts, 2 duels, and 1 zone. There is 4 stacks and 4 hooks. 2 of each for each teams.

 

The rign cacher gets you 20 points It can hold anywhere from 6 to 24 rings, the capactiy can change bettewen games.

 

The inverted post gets you 40 points.

 

The duel gets you 30 points, each team can place their rings on their repected side. The side with the most rigns wins that post.

 

The Zone, each area in the zone gives you a diffrent amount of points, the section closest to your robot is worth 5 point, the next one is worth 10 point, the one after that is 15 point and the one right under the post is 20 points. The post in the middle of the zone is worth a woping 500 points. The post in the middle can hold a total of 5.

About the Robot

Your robot cant carry more than 3 rights at a time.

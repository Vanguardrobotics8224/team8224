Events

 

Vanguard Robotics Team has hosted 3 events for towns so far with many more booked for 2025. We have 20 lego education EV3 kits for children 8-13 years old to build, code and play with. All parts are in their bins with a full color instruction booklet. The events are 1 hour long with the opertonity to expand if needed. We can do indoor and outdoor events. Weather it be for a town event or a company party we are here to help.
